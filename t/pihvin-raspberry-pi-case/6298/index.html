Inspired by @urnenfeld’s UrhoBox project, I’m working on a Raspberry Pi casing: The Pihvin
Urhopihvin translates to mighty/hero steak, vin is the Dutch spelling of (fish) fin, and it starts with Pi.

Its shape is derived from a fish model I made earlier and a Pi4 casing. The space above the Pi could house a drive. @cadaver Is that close enough to where Urho got bisected?
